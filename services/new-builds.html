Scope of Works

What We Manage

A Summerville new build is a fully managed service. We co-ordinate every professional and trade on your behalf:

  • Pre-construction liaison with architects and engineers
  • Planning application management
  • Site preparation, groundworks, and foundations
  • Structural frame — block, timber, or steel as specified
  • Roof structure, tiling or slating, and drainage
  • External cladding, render, or brickwork
  • Windows, external doors, and garage doors
  • All internal mechanical and electrical services
  • Full insulation to current BER and NZEB standards
  • Plasterwork, floor screeds, and internal joinery
  • Kitchen and bathroom fit-out (if turnkey)
  • Driveway, boundary treatment, and site clearance
  • BCAR compliance pack and Opticom sign-off
Our Standards

Built to Comply. Built to Last.

All Summerville new builds are constructed to exceed current Irish building regulations including:

Building Control (Amendment) Regulations (BCAR)

Assigned Certifier engaged on every new build project.

Nearly Zero Energy Building (NZEB)

All new builds achieve minimum A2 BER rating as standard.

HomeBond or Latent Defects Insurance

10-year structural warranty available on all new builds.

Part L Compliance

Energy performance modelling and compliance documentation provided.

How It Works

Our New Build Process

Step 01

Consultation & Brief

We meet to understand your requirements, budget, and site — then provide an indicative cost plan and programme.

Step 02

Design & Planning

Working with your architect, we develop the build specification, manage planning, and agree a construction contract.

Step 03

Construction

Your project manager runs the site to programme. You receive regular progress updates and site access at all times.

Step 04

Snagging & Handover

A thorough snagging process, followed by complete compliance documentation and key handover.
