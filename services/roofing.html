Watertight. Weatherproof. Built to Last.

Your roof is the most important protective element of your home. Whether you're building new, replacing an aging roof, or dealing with storm damage, Summerville Contractors delivers roofing that performs for decades — with careful detailing, quality materials, and meticulous workmanship.

We install natural slate, concrete and clay tile, and modern flat-roof membrane systems on new builds, extensions, and replacement projects — and handle repairs, leak diagnostics, and gutter and fascia works across Dublin and Leinster.

Scope of Works

What We Cover

From full roof installations to targeted repairs, we deliver every aspect of roofing in-house:

  • New build roof construction — timber frame to finished covering
  • Natural slate and concrete/clay tile installation
  • Flat-roof systems — EPDM, felt, and single-ply membrane
  • Full roof replacements and re-roofing
  • Storm damage repair and emergency make-safe
  • Chimney repair, lead flashing, and re-pointing
  • Fascia, soffit, and gutter renewal
  • Roof insulation upgrades (warm and cold roof)
  • Velux and rooflight integration (see Velux Installation)
  • Attic ventilation and breathable membrane detailing
