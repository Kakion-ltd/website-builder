Scope of Works

What's Included

Our house extension service covers everything from groundwork to final finish. Here's what you can expect as standard:

  • Site survey and structural engineering assessment
  • Planning application support (where required)
  • Groundworks, foundations, and drainage
  • Block work, cavity wall construction, and roof structure
  • External render or brick finish to match existing
  • Full insulation to current Building Energy Rating (BER) standards
  • Windows and external doors supplied and fitted
  • First and second fix electrical and plumbing
  • Plastering and floor screeds
  • Internal joinery — skirting, architraves, and doors
  • All compliance documentation and BCAR sign-off
Extension Types

Types We Build

We have extensive experience across all residential extension types, including:

Single-Storey Rear Extension

The most common type — kitchen, dining, or living room extension at the back of the property.

Two-Storey Extension

Maximise space with a two-storey addition — often a ground-floor kitchen plus a bedroom or bathroom above.

Side Return Extension

Uses the narrow side passage to create a wider, open-plan ground floor — popular in terraced and semi-detached homes.

Wrap-Around Extension

Combines rear and side return for the maximum possible footprint at ground level.
