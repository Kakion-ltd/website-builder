Scope of Works

What We Cover

Our renovation service is fully comprehensive. We assess the property from top to bottom and produce a detailed scope of works before a single nail is struck:

  • Structural surveys and remedial works
  • Damp proofing, tanking, and remediation
  • Full rewire — consumer unit, sockets, lighting, and smoke alarms
  • Heating system upgrade or full replacement (including heat pumps)
  • Plumbing — pipework, cylinders, and sanitary ware
  • External wall insulation (EWI) or internal insulation
  • Window and door replacement throughout
  • Full replaster — wet or dry lining as required
  • Floor screeds and floor coverings
  • Kitchen and utility supply and fit
  • Bathroom and en-suite fit-out
  • Internal joinery, painting, and final finishes

How It Works

Our Renovation Process

Step 01

Property Survey

We carry out a thorough inspection of the property and identify all works required — structural, mechanical, and cosmetic.

Step 02

Itemised Quote

A detailed written quotation covering every element of the renovation. You decide the scope — we price it clearly.

Step 03

Phased Works

Works are planned in logical sequence — structural first, services second, finishes last — to minimise disruption.

Step 04

Snag & Handover

Full walkthrough on completion. Any snags addressed immediately. All compliance certs and documentation provided.
