Scope of Works

What We Cover

Our external works service covers the full range of residential groundworks and exterior finishes:

  • Driveways — tarmac, block paving, gravel, and resin bound
  • Boundary walls — block, brick, and stone construction
  • Garden walls and feature retaining walls
  • Fencing and gate installation
  • External render — K-rend, Parex, and traditional wet dash
  • Pointing and repointing of existing brickwork
  • Soakaway and storm drainage systems
  • Patio and paving — natural stone, porcelain, and concrete flags
  • Steps and path construction
  • Landscaping groundworks and topsoil preparation
  • Garden room and outbuilding groundworks
  • Site clearance and rubble removal

External works are often the final stage of an extension or new build. We co-ordinate groundworks to follow on seamlessly from the main build — saving you time and the hassle of managing multiple contractors.
