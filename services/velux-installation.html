Velux Installation

Let Daylight In — Properly Fitted.

A Velux roof window can completely transform a room, flooding attic spaces, kitchens, and extensions with natural daylight. But a roof window is only as good as its installation — poor detailing around flashings, membranes, and insulation can lead to leaks and cold-bridging for years to come.

As certified Velux installers, Summerville fits Velux roof windows, sun tunnels, and flat-roof rooflights with correctly specified flashing kits, sealed membranes, and properly insulated reveals — so you get the daylight without the problems.

What We Install

From a single replacement window to a full banked installation, we handle every aspect:

  • Velux centre-pivot and top-hung roof windows

  • Flat-roof rooflights and walk-on glass units
  • Banked (combined) window installations
  • Like-for-like replacement of existing Velux windows
  • Correct flashing kits for slate, tile, and membrane roofs

  • Remote-control and rain-sensor opening upgrades
  • Full insulation and vapour-membrane detailing
