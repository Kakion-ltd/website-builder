Carpentry

Timber Work Done Properly.

Carpentry is at the heart of every great build. From the first timbers of a new floor or roof structure, to the final architrave and skirting in a finished room, quality carpentry is what gives a home its feel — straight lines, tight joints, and details that look as good up close as they do from across the room.

Our in-house carpenters work across new builds, extensions, renovations, and attic conversions — and take on bespoke joinery commissions such as built-in wardrobes, storage, and feature staircases.

Scope of Works

What We Cover

Our carpentry service spans every stage of the build:

  • First-fix: floor joists, roof trusses, stud partitions
  • Structural timber frames and engineered timber installations
  • Door lining, window boards, and noggins
  • Second-fix: skirting, architrave, and mouldings
  • Internal and external door hanging
  • Staircase installation and balustrade detail
  • Bespoke built-in wardrobes and storage solutions
  • Kitchen carcass installation and handover for fit-out
  • Decking, pergolas, and external timber structures
  • Timber cladding — internal and external
