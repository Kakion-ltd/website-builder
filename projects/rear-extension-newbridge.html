Rear Single-Storey Extension

Newbridge, Co. Kildare — A lean-to rear extension creating a bright open-plan kitchen and dining room with sliding glass doors to the garden.

Project Overview

More Room, More Light, More Life

A 34m² lean-to rear extension in Newbridge — exempt from planning permission — more than doubling the kitchen-dining area of a 1990s semi-detached. A polycarbonate opal roof panel floods the dining space with diffused natural light, and slim-profile aluminium sliding doors open to a level concrete patio at garden level.

Completed in 12 weeks on a fixed-price contract with no variations.

  • Strip foundation and insulated concrete slab
  • Lean-to monopitch roof — fibre cement slates with polycarbonate panel
  • Aluminium sliding doors — 2.4m opening to garden
  • Underfloor heating throughout — new zone from existing boiler
  • Polished concrete screed — continuous from extension to existing kitchen
  • New kitchen units and worktop — supply and installation
  • Level patio slab outside sliding doors
