Master Suite Attic Conversion

Swords, Co. Dublin — A dormer attic conversion creating a private master bedroom with en-suite and walk-in wardrobe, exempt from planning permission.

Project Overview

A Private Retreat Above the Roofline

A rear dormer attic conversion in Swords creating a 38m² master suite with en-suite and walk-in wardrobe — exempt from planning permission. Two LVL structural beams were introduced to carry the new floor, and a space-saving quarter-turn stair was threaded through the existing landing cupboard, preserving the room layout below.

Completed in 8 weeks with minimal disruption to the occupied household. BCAR compliance certificate issued at completion.

  • LVL ridge and mid-span beam installation
  • Rear dormer — clad in fibre cement slates
  • Two Velux FK06 roof windows to front elevation
  • Quarter-turn stair through existing landing cupboard
  • En-suite bathroom — shower, WC, and vanity
  • Walk-in wardrobe with fitted shelving and rail system
  • Full insulation, plasterboard, and decoration throughout
