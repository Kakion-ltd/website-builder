Three-Bed Semi-Detached New Build

Ashbourne, Co. Meath — A turnkey A-rated semi-detached family home built on an infill site in an established residential estate.

Project Overview

A New Home in an Established Neighbourhood

A turnkey semi-detached new build on an infill site in Ashbourne, Co. Meath — delivered on a design-and-build basis alongside the client's architect. Building between two occupied properties required careful sequencing and party wall agreements, while a high water table required a modified foundation design, both identified and priced before contract with no surprises.

Completed in 9 months with an A2 BER rating. HomeBond warranty, BCAR compliance documents, and an Opticom BER certificate provided at handover — supporting the client's SEAI grant applications.

  • Modified strip foundations with tanked sub-floor void
  • Two-storey block cavity wall — 150mm full-fill insulation
  • Hipped cut roof — concrete interlocking tiles
  • Air-source heat pump, underfloor heating, and MVHR
  • Full electrical, plumbing, plaster, and screeds
  • Fitted kitchen, two bathrooms, and all internal joinery
  • Block paved driveway, boundary walls, and rear lawn
