Open-Plan Kitchen Extension

Greystones, Co. Wicklow — A rear single-storey extension transforming a dated kitchen into a bright, open-plan living and dining space.

Project Overview

Bringing the Garden In

A 42m² rear single-storey extension in Greystones, opening the entire ground floor rear into a cohesive kitchen, dining, and living space. A 3m × 1.5m aluminium roof lantern is the centrepiece — flooding the space with natural light year-round — while 4.2m anthracite bifold doors connect it directly to the garden in warmer months.

Completed in 14 weeks on programme and within budget. Compliance documentation issued at handover.

  • Structural 5.8m steel beam opening the full ground floor rear
  • Pad foundations and slab floor with wet underfloor heating
  • Warm flat roof with aluminium roof lantern (3m × 1.5m)
  • Slim-profile aluminium bifold doors — 4.2m, powder-coated anthracite
  • Large-format porcelain tiles — continuous from inside to patio
  • Full electrical and co-ordination with client's kitchen fitter
  • Patio and garden step construction at new floor level
