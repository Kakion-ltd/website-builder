Two-Storey Side Extension

Lucan, Co. Dublin — A two-storey side extension adding a ground-floor home office and a fourth bedroom above, matching the existing house finish throughout.

Project Overview

Two Floors of New Space, Seamlessly Added

A two-storey side extension on a 3.1m side return in Lucan, adding a dedicated home office at ground floor and a fourth bedroom above — 68m² across two levels. The extension is effectively indistinguishable from the original house, with colour-matched K-rend render, matching roof tiles, and windows throughout.

Completed over 20 weeks with full planning permission. BCAR compliance documentation issued at practical completion.

  • Strip foundation alongside existing footings
  • Two-storey block cavity wall — 300mm full-fill insulation
  • Cut roof matched to existing pitch — colour-matched tiles
  • K-rend render matched to existing — sampled and signed off on site
  • Ground floor: home office with built-in desk and storage
  • First floor: double bedroom with fitted wardrobe
  • Full electrical, heating connections, plaster, and decoration
