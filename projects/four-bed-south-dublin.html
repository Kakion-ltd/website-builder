Four-Bed Detached

South Dublin — A complete turnkey new build delivered from foundation to final fit-out, on a tight suburban site.

Project Overview

A Family Home Built to Last

A turnkey 4-bed detached new build in South Dublin, delivered over 11 months from site clearance to full fit-out. The restricted site required careful co-ordination with the structural engineer, while the A2 BER specification meant integrating a ground-source heat pump, MVHR system, and triple-glazed windows across the build programme.

Full BCAR compliance documentation, an Opticom BER certificate, and a 10-year HomeBond structural warranty were provided at handover.

  • Site clearance, strip foundations, and two-storey block cavity walls
  • Cut roof — natural slate, lead flashings, and EPDM flat sections
  • Triple-glazed windows and external doors — Irish Oak finish
  • Ground-source heat pump, underfloor heating, and MVHR
  • Full electrical, plumbing, wet plaster, and floor screeds
  • Fitted kitchen, utility, and four bathrooms — supply and fit
  • Block paved driveway, boundary walls, and garden landscaping
