Four-Bed Detached

South Dublin — A complete turnkey new build delivered from foundation to final fit-out, on a tight suburban site.

Project Overview

A Family Home Built to Last

This four-bedroom detached residence in South Dublin was commissioned by a young family looking to build their forever home on an existing site inherited from family. The brief was clear: a modern, energy-efficient family home that would blend naturally with the character of the established neighbourhood while meeting the very latest building standards.

Summerville Contractors was appointed as the main contractor following a competitive tender process. Working alongside the client's appointed architect and structural engineer, we managed every stage of the build from site clearance through to full interior fit-out — acting as a single point of accountability throughout.

The Challenge

The plot presented several challenges: restricted site access from a narrow laneway, proximity to neighbouring boundary walls requiring careful shoring, and a requirement to complete all groundworks during a dry weather window before the autumn rains. Careful programme planning and close co-ordination with the structural engineer allowed us to work through these constraints without delay.

The client also specified a high-performance building envelope to achieve an A2 BER rating — requiring triple-glazed windows, an MVHR (mechanical ventilation with heat recovery) system, and a ground-source heat pump. Integrating these systems during the build required detailed co-ordination between our electrical, plumbing, and insulation sub-teams.

Scope of Works

  • Site clearance, topographical survey, and setting out
  • Excavation and reinforced concrete strip foundations
  • Two-storey block cavity wall construction with full fill insulation
  • Cut roof with natural slate, lead flashings, and EPDM flat roof sections
  • Triple-glazed windows and external doors — Irish Oak finish
  • K-rend silicone render externally, traditional stone sills
  • Ground-source heat pump, underfloor heating, and MVHR system
  • Full first and second fix electrical and plumbing
  • Wet plaster throughout, floor screeds, and ceramic floor tiles
  • Fitted kitchen, utility room, and four bathrooms — full supply and fit
  • Internal joinery — staircase, skirting, architraves, and fitted wardrobes
  • Block paved driveway, boundary walls, and garden landscaping

The Outcome

The project was completed on programme over 11 months from commencement to handover. The home achieved an A2 BER rating as specified, with projected annual heating costs of under €600. The clients were particularly pleased with the quality of the internal joinery and the seamless integration of the building services, which are largely invisible once the house is furnished.

Full BCAR compliance documentation, an Opticom BER certificate, and a 10-year HomeBond structural warranty were provided at handover.
