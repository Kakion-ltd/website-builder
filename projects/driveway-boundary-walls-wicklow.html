Driveway & Boundary Walls

Bray, Co. Wicklow — Full driveway replacement in block paving, new rendered boundary walls, and electric gate installation for a detached home.

Project Overview

Kerb Appeal, Properly Done

A complete driveway and boundary overhaul for a detached home in Bray. The existing tarmac was broken out and replaced with Tobermore Tegula block paving in herringbone, the cracked concrete boundary walls demolished and rebuilt with K-rend render colour-matched to the house, and electric swing gates with intercom installed. The house front was re-rendered at the same time, delivering a consistent finish across the whole frontage.

Completed in three weeks.

  • Break-out of existing tarmac and compacted hardcore sub-base
  • Tobermore Tegula block paving — herringbone with contrasting border
  • Drainage channel at entrance
  • New concrete block boundary walls — 1.2m with soldier course capping
  • K-rend smooth render — colour-matched to house front
  • Electric swing gates — powder-coated anthracite steel with intercom
  • LED coach lights to gate piers
