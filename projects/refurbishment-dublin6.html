Full Internal Refurbishment

Ranelagh, Dublin 6 — A complete strip-back and refurbishment of a Victorian terraced house, retaining all original features while delivering a thoroughly modern interior.

Project Overview

Victorian Character, Modern Comfort

A full strip-back and refurbishment of a Victorian terrace in Ranelagh — stripped to bare brickwork, fully rewired and replumbed, and rebuilt to a high standard. Original features were carefully restored rather than replaced: ceiling cornices repaired with lime plaster, cast iron fireplaces stripped and refinished, and the Victorian dog-leg staircase restored with matched replacement balusters.

Completed in 16 weeks. BER improved from E2 to C3. Leased at a premium rental within two weeks of handover.

  • Full internal strip-out and structural remedial works
  • Full rewire, replumb, and A-rated condensing boiler
  • Lime-based breathable plaster throughout
  • Cornice restoration (×2 rooms) and fireplace restoration (×3)
  • Staircase restoration — replacement balusters and handrail repair
  • Engineered oak flooring and shaker kitchen
  • Three bathrooms and full redecoration
