Period Home Renovation

Naas, Co. Kildare — A comprehensive renovation of a 1930s semi-detached, tackling damp, rewiring, and a full interior refurbishment from top to bottom.

Project Overview

Restoring Character, Adding Comfort

A comprehensive renovation of a 1930s semi-detached in Naas — damp treatment, full rewire and replumb, internal wall insulation, and a complete fit-out from new kitchen and bathrooms to engineered oak floors. Original period features including the staircase, cast iron fireplaces, and cornicing were carefully retained throughout.

Completed in 18 weeks. BER improved from F to C1.

  • Damp-proof injection and renovating plaster system throughout
  • Internal wall insulation (IWI) — mineral wool, vapour barrier, plasterboard
  • Full rewire — new consumer unit, LED downlights
  • Full replumb — pressurised system, A-rated gas boiler
  • Engineered oak floor — front reception and hallway
  • New fitted kitchen and three bathrooms
  • Full redecoration and external render repair
