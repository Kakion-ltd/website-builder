Who We Are

Built on Reputation. Driven by Craft.

Summerville Contractors Ltd was founded to deliver residential construction that stands the test of time. What started as a small family operation has grown into one of the most trusted building contractors in the Dublin region.

Our business is built on referrals, repeat work, and tradespeople who take genuine pride in their craft — from single-storey extensions to complete new builds, always with the same meticulous standards.
