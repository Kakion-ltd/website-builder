About Summerville Contractors Ltd

A family-run construction business built on craftsmanship, integrity, and a reputation earned over 15 years of delivering exceptional results across Ireland.

Who We Are

Built on Reputation. Driven by Craft.

Summerville Contractors Ltd was founded with a single purpose: to deliver residential construction projects that stand the test of time. What started as a small family operation has grown into one of the most trusted building contractors in the Dublin region — without ever losing the personal touch that defines who we are.

We've never grown through advertising alone. Our business is built on referrals from satisfied clients, repeat work from homeowners who trust us, and a team of dedicated tradespeople who take genuine pride in their craft. Every nail, every brick, every finish reflects the standards we set from day one.

Today, Summerville manages projects ranging from single-storey house extensions to complete new builds — always with the same meticulous approach, transparent communication, and accountability that our clients have come to expect.

Credentials

Registered, Insured & Compliant

We meet every professional and regulatory requirement for residential construction in Ireland. When you engage Summerville, you're working with a company that operates to the highest standards of compliance and accountability.

  • Registered with the Construction Industry Register Ireland (CIRI)
  • Public liability insurance — fully up to date
  • Employer's liability insurance in place for all operatives
  • VAT registered — invoices issued in accordance with Irish Revenue requirements
  • All works completed to Building Control (Amendment) Regulations (BCAR) standards
  • Compliance certs and documentation provided at project handover
  • Safe Pass and Manual Handling certification across entire workforce
