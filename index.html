About Summerville

A Family Business Built on Reputation

Summerville Contractors Ltd is a family-run residential construction company with over 15 years of experience delivering exceptional builds across Dublin and surrounding counties. We've grown entirely on word-of-mouth and repeat business — a testament to the quality of our work and the trust of our clients.

Every project, from a modest kitchen extension to a full turnkey new build, is treated with the same level of care. Our team of skilled tradespeople are fully employed and vetted, ensuring consistent quality from day one.
